Hubot

Branding; Visual Identity

Project Info

Hubot is a robot shop that sells a wide range of “high IQ” toys. The assignment of this project is to present its unique programming feature in a way that is amusing but not overly rigid. For the logo, common symbols in the program replace the letter “o”, and at the same time they represent the four brand concepts: play, programming, discovery and sharing. Meanwhile, the selection of colors and the arrangement of the dots make Hubot more futuristic.

Hubot是一家编程机器人玩具店，售卖各式各样的“高智商”玩具。这个项目的任务在于如何表现出它独特的编程属性同时又不失有趣，避免过于死板。标识使用了程序中常见的符号来代替字母o，代表了游戏，编程，发现与共享四个品牌概念。同时，颜色的选择与点阵状的排列方式使Hubot更具未来感。
