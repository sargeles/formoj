Meet

Branding; Visual Identity

Project Info

Meet is a Korean barbecue restaurant targeting young people. Our assignment is to create an art direction and a series of posters to express the vivid brand image and promote the various meats. How to introduce meats into the visual system but not to give people a greasy feel is the key problem we face. The shapes of meats on the grill naturally present geometries, beef tongue as circle, pork neck as rounded rectangle, and beef rolls as cylinder. This idea lead us to the milestone of our concept, connecting different meats with specified geometries. Adding patterns is an abstract way of representing dressing the bbq meat, as well as giving the geometries a playful and hot final touch. We also take advantage of the most basic graphical element - grid, to represent grills, which is unexpected but also reasonable.

Meet是一家面向年轻人的韩国烤肉店。客户委托我们为其品牌建立一套视觉方向以及设计一套海报来传达Meet年轻热烈的品牌形象。肉的种类繁多，怎么巧妙地引入烤肉的燥热感却又避免烤肉油腻的负面形象是首要解决的视觉难题。圆形的牛舌、圆角矩形的猪颈肉和卷筒型的调味牛肉，将不同的肉和几何体联系在一起是我们概念的第一步。再者，图案的加入，不仅仅是为烤肉加调味这一过程的抽象化表达，也为这个视觉体系加入了趣味和活泼。我们还意料之外又情理之中的使用了非常平面感觉的网格元素来表示烤肉架。整个视觉方向让Meet热情活泼的特质呼之欲出。
