Pacha

Product; Branding; Visual Identity

Project Info

Pacha is a conceptual chocolate product. We enlarged the feeling at the moment of breaking off the chocolate bar. It would bring you uncomfortable feeling unexpectedly. Chocolate was composed by simple geometric graphics. The connections seemed like weak and fragile. Pacha! The chocolate was designed to break at the other position surprisedly when you broke it. Compared with other geometric graphics, the left imperfect part made consumers feel weird, enhanced the unconsciousness of geometry perfection for evoking the further unexpected feeling.
