Uniroom

Advertising; Video; Animation

Project Info

The animation is made for an LA based company called Uniroom, which is an online marketplace and hospitality service for people to lease or rent the apartment for short and long term. The video is created to explain how it works different and more convenient than the other similar apartment rental companies.

这部动画广告是为洛杉矶一家名为Uniroom的公司制作的。Uniroom是一家线上酒店服务公司，为人们提供短期或长期租赁公寓的服务。这段视频解释了它是如何不同于其他类似的租赁公司，并且更方便。
